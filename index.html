Trust + Holding LLC Structure

The structure I’m building around my business is designed to think long-term: operating companies make money, a holding company protects ownership, and a trust can help organize legacy planning.

Operating LLC

The operating LLC runs the daily business. This is where the food trailer, services, sales, vendors, employees, and customer activity happen.

Holding LLC

The holding LLC is designed to hold ownership interests, intellectual property, equipment, or other valuable business assets separate from day-to-day risk.

Trust Planning

A trust can be used as part of a long-term plan to organize ownership, succession, family control, and generational transfer.

Protection Mindset

The goal is not shortcuts. The goal is clean records, separate entities, written agreements, insurance, compliance, and smart risk management.
