Official Filing Fees + Required Documents

Our Fee Is Separate From State Filing Fees

Our prices are service fees for guidance, preparation, organization, branding, setup help, and convenience. Government filing fees, Secretary of State fees, permits, licenses, domain fees, hosting fees, tax fees, third-party provider fees, and professional legal/tax review are separate unless specifically stated in writing.

  • Ohio LLC filing fee: Ohio currently lists the domestic LLC Articles of Organization filing fee as $99.
  • Operating LLC + Holding LLC example: If your structure requires two separate Ohio LLCs, expect two state filings: $99 + $99 = $198 in state filing fees before our service fee or optional extras.
  • Articles of Organization: This is the official LLC formation document filed with the Ohio Secretary of State. It is different from Articles of Incorporation, which are for corporations.
  • Business banking: Banks usually need approved Articles of Organization, EIN confirmation, owner ID, business address/contact info, and sometimes an operating agreement before opening a business account.
  • Bank appointment setup: We can help prepare the appointment process after verifying the required documents are available.
  • Other possible requirements: vendor license / sales tax registration, local permits, food permits, zoning, insurance, EIN, statutory agent, domain, hosting, business email, and professional review may apply depending on the business.
  • Verify before paying: Always verify current filing fees directly with the Ohio Secretary of State before filing.
